# Blackjack Game

Welcome to the Blackjack Game! This is a simple web-based version of the classic card game, built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The game allows you to play against the dealer and test your luck and strategy.

## Features

- **User-Friendly Interface**: The game has a clean and intuitive layout, making it easy for players to understand the rules and gameplay.
- **Interactive Gameplay**: Players can hit, stand, or double down, making strategic decisions based on their hand and the dealer's visible card.
- **Responsive Design**: The game is designed to be responsive, ensuring a good user experience on both desktop and mobile devices.
- **Score Tracking**: Keep track of your wins and losses during each game session.

## How to Play

1. **Start the Game**: Click the "Start" button to begin a new game.
2. **Make Your Move**:
   - **Hit**: Draw another card to improve your hand.
   - **Stand**: Keep your current hand and end your turn.
   - **Double Down**: Double your bet and receive only one additional card.
3. **Dealer's Turn**: After you finish your turn, the dealer will reveal their cards and play according to standard rules.
4. **Determine the Winner**: Compare your hand to the dealer's hand to see who wins!
